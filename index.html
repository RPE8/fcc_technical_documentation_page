Conquest Hold

Conquest Hold is the main Horde camp in the Grizzly Hills. Conqueror Krenna, known to be aggressive even for an Orc, rules over the forces stationed there. After a certain point, Gorgonna and Krenna get in a fight with the player caught in the middle and in the quest The Conquest Pit: Final Showdown a Krenna is killed. After her death, Gorgonna becomes the new leader of Conquest Hold.

Barracks serves as the local inn.

Drakil'jin Ruins

The Drakil'jin Ruins can be found south of Thor Modan and east of Westfall Brigade Encampment in Grizzly Hills.

It serves as a burial ground for members of the Drakkari Empire and its ancient kings. Barracks serves as the local inn.
